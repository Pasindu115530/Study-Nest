Welcome to Study Nest

Study Nest is a centralized web-based platform designed to streamline access to university course materials for students across all departments. It offers a structured repository where students can find lecture notes, past papers, presentations, and more, all organized by course and semester.

Our Objectives

  • Centralize and preserve academic resources.
  • Provide easy and secure access to course materials.
  • Enable smooth navigation between departments and courses.
  • Support students in long-term academic planning and revision.

Key Features

The platform includes user authentication, role-based access (student/admin), and downloadable content. Users can browse materials by department and semester, and admins can update or manage the materials.
