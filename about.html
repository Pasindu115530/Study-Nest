Welcome to Study Nest

Our Objectives

  • Centralize and preserve academic resources.
  • Provide secure and easy access to study content.
  • Enable smooth navigation by department and semester.
  • Support long-term academic planning and revisions.

Key Features

  • User authentication with student/admin roles.
  • Downloadable PDFs, slides, and past papers.
  • Department-wise and semester-wise filtering.
  • Mobile responsive user interface.
  • Secure and reliable access 24/7.
  • Admin tools for content upload and updates.
